About

Creating spaces that are functional, as well as aesthetically pleasing, is the specialty of Milton Design Inc.

Your project needs can be accommodated with a full range of services including preliminary concept drawings to complete project management of construction.

Our Motto at is Milton Design Inc. takes pride in integrating facilities & people with exemplary service

We are certified in NCIDQ, Bill 124 Small Building and PMP

Services

Feasibility Planning

  • Analyzing user’s needs, goals and life and safety requirements
  • Developing preliminary design concepts that are functional and financially appropriate
  • Ensuring compliance with corporate building and maintenance standards, such as Accessibility Design Guidelines (AODA) and Leadership in Environmental Design (LEED).

Design and Material Sourcing

  • Sourcing finish materials for schedules, specifications, as well as millwork details for construction
  • Creating specification drawings for interior construction, materials, finishes, space planning, furnishings, fixtures and equipment
  • Proposing comprehensive new workplace space accommodation initiatives based on physical building attributes

Construction Drawings for Permit and Tender

  • Preparing permit/tender drawings and specifications relating to all interior architectural design aspects of assigned project
  • Collaborating with other professionals who offer services in the technical areas of mechanical, architectural, electrical, and structural design as required for regulatory approval (building permits)
  • Monitoring and ensuring record drawings and documentation (soft and hard files) relating to Building Permit are maintained accurate and up-to-date.
  • Preparing Request for Proposals, Request for Quotations and Tender Documents for consultant and Contractor selection.
  • Responding to bidder enquiries, preparing addenda, and conducting Bidder's site meetings.
  • Facilitating project related meetings and liaising with client representatives and project partners, until project completion
